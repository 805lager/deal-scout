Deal Scout — AI Deal Scorer

Summary from package

AI-powered deal scoring for Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, and Amazon. Scores deals instantly using real market data.

Description
Deal Scout scores Facebook Marketplace listings instantly using AI — so you know if a deal is worth it before you message the seller.

HOW IT WORKS
Open any Facebook Marketplace listing and Deal Scout automatically:
- Scores the deal 1–10 using real eBay sold prices as the market benchmark
- Analyzes the listing photo with AI vision to catch condition mismatches
- Flags scam signals and seller trust issues
- Shows where to buy new if the used price isn't worth the risk
- Recommends a fair counter-offer price

WHAT YOU SEE
✅ BUY / ⚠️ CAUTION / ❌ AVOID verdict with score
📊 Market comparison — sold avg, active listings, new retail price
🔍 AI summary — green flags, red flags, condition notes
🛡️ Security score — scam risk assessment
🛒 Where to buy — ranked affiliate cards (Amazon, eBay, Back Market, etc.)

COMPLETELY FREE
No subscription. No account required. Just install and browse.

PRIVACY
Deal Scout never stores your browsing history or personal data. Listing data is sent only to score the deal and is not retained.

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS
- Facebook Marketplace (full scoring)
- Craigslist — coming soon
- OfferUp — coming soon
