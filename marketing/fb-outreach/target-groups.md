# Facebook Group Outreach Targets

Initial outbound list. Mix of buy/sell, reseller, deal-hunting, and category-specific groups. Search Facebook for the exact name in your area before posting — most are city-scoped or have multiple chapters.

## Tier 1 — Reseller / flipping (highest intent)
1. **Reseller Community** (general flipping advice, very active)
2. **Flipping Side Hustle** (eBay, Amazon, Marketplace flippers)
3. **Garage Sale Flippers** (resellers sourcing locally)
4. **Reselling for Profit** (mixed-category resellers)
5. **Marketplace Flippers** (FBM-specific reselling)

## Tier 2 — Deal-hunting / frugal (high intent)
6. **Frugal Living Tips & Tricks**
7. **Extreme Couponing & Deal Hunters**
8. **Deal Hunters** (general)
9. **Cheap Finds & Hidden Gems**

## Tier 3 — General buy/sell (medium intent, very large audiences)
10. **[Your City] Buy Nothing / Buy Sell Trade** (search your metro)
11. **[Your City] Marketplace Finds**
12. **Facebook Marketplace Tips & Tricks**
13. **Marketplace Deals USA**

## Tier 4 — Category-specific (high relevance for category collectors)
14. **Watch Collectors / Watch Buy Sell Trade** (Tudor/Rolex/Seiko buyers)
15. **Acoustic Guitar Lovers** (Taylor, Martin, Gibson buyers)
16. **Vintage Audio / Speaker Collectors** (Polk, JBL, Pioneer buyers)
17. **Apple Trading Post / Mac & iPad Buy Sell** (eBay tech buyers)
18. **HVAC Used Equipment** (window AC / portable AC buyers)

## Tier 5 — Adjacent communities (broad awareness)
19. **r/flipping crossposters / "Flipping Reddit Migration"**
20. **Side Hustle Nation / Side Hustle Ideas**

---

## Posting protocol
1. Read pinned rules before posting. If self-promo isn't allowed, only do organic comment replies (see comment-templates.md) and never drop the link unless asked.
2. Always DM admins of Tier 3 / Tier 4 groups before posting (see dm-templates.md).
3. Lead with the helpful tip; the install link comes second.
4. Track which groups convert in a simple spreadsheet — double down on the top 3, drop the bottom 5 after 30 days.
