# Facebook Group Comment Templates

For organic replies to common posts in buy/sell, deal-hunting, and reseller groups. Goal: be genuinely helpful first; the link comes only if asked.

---

## Comment 1 — "Is this a good price?" / "What's this worth?"

> Quick way to check: look up the model on eBay and filter by Sold Listings (the slider near the bottom of the filter sidebar). The average of the last 10–20 sold prices is your real market value — asking prices are usually 20–40% higher.
>
> If you want a shortcut, there's a free Chrome extension called Deal Scout that does this automatically right on the listing page — happy to drop a link if helpful.

---

## Comment 2 — "What apps / tools do you all use?"

> For pricing I use Deal Scout — free Chrome extension, scores Marketplace, eBay, Craigslist, and OfferUp listings using real eBay sold prices. Saves me from opening 5 tabs every time. No account, no ads, just install and it works.

---

## Comment 3 — "How do you avoid getting scammed on Marketplace?"

> A few rules that have saved me:
> 1. Reverse-image search the photos — stolen photos are the #1 tell
> 2. Be suspicious of any listing >25% under market for the category
> 3. Never pay before you see it in person; never click payment links sent in chat
>
> I also run a free Chrome extension I built called Deal Scout that flags a lot of this automatically — under-market pricing, condition mismatches between photo and description, etc. Glad to share if useful.
