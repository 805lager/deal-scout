## Detailed description

Deal Scout scores secondhand listings using AI — so you know if a deal is worth it before you message the seller.

HOW IT WORKS
Open any Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, eBay, or OfferUp listing and Deal Scout automatically:

• Scores the deal 1–10 using real eBay sold prices as the market benchmark
• Analyzes listing photos with AI vision to catch condition mismatches
• Checks product reputation (model reliability, common issues, owner reviews)
• Compares prices across multiple marketplaces and retailers
• Flags scam signals and seller trust issues
• Shows where to buy new if the used price isn't worth the risk
• Recommends a fair counter-offer price
• Generates ready-to-send negotiation messages in three tones

WHAT YOU SEE
✅ BUY / ⚠️ CAUTION / ❌ AVOID verdict with score
📊 Market comparison — sold avg, active listings, new retail price
⭐ Product reputation — model reliability and common issues
🔍 AI summary — green flags, red flags, condition notes
🛡️ Security score — scam risk assessment
🛒 Where to buy — ranked cards across Amazon, eBay, and more
💬 Negotiation messages — polite, direct, or lowball, copy in one click
🔖 Save listings — revisit later from the toolbar with price-drop tracking

COMPLETELY FREE
No subscription. No account required. No ads. Just install and browse.

PRIVACY
Deal Scout doesn't track your browsing, doesn't sell your data, and doesn't require a login. Listing data is sent only to score the deal and is not retained.

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS
• Facebook Marketplace
• Craigslist
• OfferUp
• eBay

GET STARTED
Install Deal Scout, open the next used listing you're considering, and the score panel appears automatically. That's it.
