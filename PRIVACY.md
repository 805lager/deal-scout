# Deal Scout — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## What we collect

Deal Scout does **not** collect, store, or share personal data.

When you view a Facebook Marketplace listing, Deal Scout sends the following data to a scoring API to generate a deal score:
- Listing title, price, description, and condition
- Listing photo URL (for AI vision analysis)
- Approximate location (city/region from the listing, not your device location)

This data is used solely to produce the deal score and is **not retained, logged, or associated with any user identity** after the score is returned.

## What we do not collect

- Browsing history
- Personal information (name, email, address)
- Financial or payment data
- Authentication credentials
- Any data from pages outside of Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, or Amazon

## Local storage

Deal Scout stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:
- Your API endpoint preference
- Price history for listings you have viewed (stored only on your device, never transmitted)

## Third parties

Deal Scout does not sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties.

Affiliate links (Amazon, eBay, etc.) are generated based on the listing category. Clicking an affiliate link is subject to the privacy policy of that retailer.
